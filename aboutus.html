ABOUT US

Created based on the concept of exclusivity

SEASONLESS STYLE TO HAVE AND TO HOLD ON TO: THE NINE-WORD SOL'EACE MANIFESTO.


Arusa features timeless furniture, with natural fabrics, curved lines, plenty of mirrors and classic design, which can be incorporated into any decor project. The pieces enchant for their sobriety, to last for generations, faithful to the shapes of each period, with a touch of the present.

The pieces stand out for their contemporary straight lines and imposing presence. Current, following the world trend of the great masters, the furniture stands out for its noble and innovative materials, composing sophisticated and exclusive environments.

About our legacy

Elegant and welcoming, it has a selected mix of products, carefully thought out to optimize the moment of project specification, regardless of their dimensions or styles. Our gateway to the Sol'ace universe is a great opportunity to acquire contemporary pieces, with world-wide quality and design.
Conceived with the objective of displaying the most diverse concepts of life style, when it comes to bedrooms, the store features exclusive furniture for the most intimate areas of the house, such as beds, mattresses and nightstands, as well as a special selection of wallpapers, fabrics and trousseaus. With a mix of possibilities and high customization, the furniture meets any expectations, composing cozy and charming rooms
